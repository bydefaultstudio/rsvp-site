Unordered List
  • Unordered lists help organize related items without hierarchy
  • Perfect for showing features, benefits, or options
  • Allows readers to quickly scan through content
  • Keep items concise and parallel in structure
Ordered List
  1. Use headings to create a visual hierarchy.
  2. Structure content with clear lists.
  3. Guide readers step-by-step with ordered items.
  4. Keep everything neat and easy to follow.
